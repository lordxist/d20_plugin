create_with_planned_generation(attributes = nil)

Adds the planned generation of ability scores to the basic create method of GameCharacter. To create a character with this method, assume a basic score of 8 for each score, and increase the scores you want to.

Example:

GameCharacter.create(:str => 9, :dex => 8)

Spending more than the total amount of score points will result in returning nil.
